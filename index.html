Our Story

A tiny counter, an honest cup, and no rush to finish it.

UBS Coffee started as a single covered stall along a quiet row of shophouses — a folding table, a few stacked chairs, and a promise to keep the coffee simple and the waffles fresh off the iron. It's grown since, but that first idea never left: good coffee doesn't need to be complicated, and a good waffle should always be eaten warm.

Today the counter is still small, the crowd is still mostly regulars, and there's usually a tiny figurine or two keeping watch over the espresso machine. We keep a stack of well-loved card games behind the counter too, for anyone in no rush to leave. Stop by, we'll have your cup ready before you've picked a seat.
